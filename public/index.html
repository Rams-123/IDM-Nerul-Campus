About the Campus

ITM IDM Nerul has secured its position as a leader in offering Design and Media Studies.

The cutting-edge campus offers the right space and facilities to help your creativity flow. IDM Nerul campus is a modern facility, ideal for learning designing and media with it many classrooms, CAD labs, drape studios, and more. Take advantage of its latest infrastructure that facilitates hands-on learning, concurrent soft-skills training and industry connects while you explore your creative niche.

Key Highlights

  • CAD labs where students can learn and practice on the latest software for VFX and Animation
  • Campus bus facility, meant to ferry between Nerul Station and the campus, is available for all students and staff members
  • Landscaped grounds with greenery around will take you away from city pollution and close to nature
